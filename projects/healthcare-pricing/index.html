The project

Turning inconsistent healthcare records into reliable cost estimates.

The project pairs a rigorous preprocessing and regression pipeline with clear visual analysis so model performance and pricing patterns can be communicated to stakeholders.

50K+records processed
>.85model R²

Approach

I cleaned and prepared more than 50,000 records by imputing missing values, encoding categorical variables, and scaling features. I then compared regression approaches with scikit-learn and XGBoost, evaluating the results with MAE, RMSE, and R².

What I delivered

  • An end-to-end preprocessing and regression pipeline
  • An XGBoost model achieving an R² above 0.85
  • A 25% reduction in training time through improved preprocessing
  • matplotlib analysis and Tableau dashboards for stakeholder presentation
